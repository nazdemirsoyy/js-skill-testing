Mustfa Kemal Atatürk

Father of the Turks

Here's a time line of Mustfa Kemal Atatürk life:

  • 1881 - Born in Salonica
  • 1893 - He entered a military highschool where his mathematics teacher gave him the second name Kemal (meaning perfection)in recognition of young Mustafa's superior achievement. He was thereafter known as MustafaKemal.
  • 1905 - Mustafa Kemal graduated from the War Academy in Istanbul with the rank of Staff Captain.
  • 1915 - When Dardanelles campaign was launched, Colonel Mustafa Kemal became anational hero by winning successive victories and finally repelling the invaders. Promoted to general in 1916, at age 35, he liberated two major provinces in eastern Turkey that year.
  • 1919 - On May 19, Mustafa Kemal Pasha landed in the Black Sea port of Samsun to startthe War of Independence.
  • 1920 - On April 23, the Grand National Assembly was inaugurated. Mustafa Kemal Pasha was elected to its Presidency.
  • 1923 - Atatürk married Latife Usakligil in early 1923. The marriage ended in divorce in 1925.
  • 1923 - July 1923, the national government signed the Lausanne Treaty with Great Britain,France, Greece, Italy, and others. In mid-October, Ankara became the capital of the newTurkish State. On October 29, the Republic was proclaimed and Mustafa Kemal Pasha wasunanimously elected President of the Republic.
  • 1934 - When the surname law was adopted, the national parliament gave him the name "Atatürk" (Father of the Turks).
  • 1938 - On November 10, 1938, following an illness of a few months, the national liberator and the Father of modern Turkey died.
  • 1953 - Atatürk's body was re-interred in this splendid mausoleum.
